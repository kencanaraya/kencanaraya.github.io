ABOUT

Our company was established in 2017 and is located in Jakarta, Indonesia. The company's main line of work is contracting and supplying defence equipment throughout Indonesia

The company is committed to providing a high-quality defence product. The company is also an authorized reseller of ATN optical products based in America

VISION

To become a leading company in the procurement and distribution of defence equipment in Indonesia

MISSION

  • Assisting and supporting the national defence and security of Indonesia
  • Conducting the business of goods with integrated technology
  • Providing high-quality defence equipment consulting services by using the appropriate practices and technology
  • Applying the of good corporate governance and continuously making improvements
  • Establishing a good relationship with suppliers and buyers while also putting honesty at the forefront of our business
